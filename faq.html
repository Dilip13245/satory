General Information & Booking

We are conveniently located at 928 Ferry Road, Galveston, TX 77550, just minutes from the UTMB campus and downtown.

Our community features modern 1-bedroom / 1-bath apartments, including select larger layouts. Certain one-bedroom units can be arranged for shared living, making them ideal for roommates looking to balance comfort and affordability.

The Landing was completely reimagined and fully renovated in 2026. Every unit features high-end appliances, modern finishes, and all-new interiors designed for contemporary island living.

The Landing is ideally located near some of Galveston’s most important institutions and destinations. The community sits within walking distance or a short bike ride of UTMB, providing convenient access to its medical campus, hospital, and academic facilities. Galveston College and Texas A&M University at Galveston are also easily accessible, making The Landing a practical home base for students, faculty, and professionals alike.

Beyond work and school, residents are just minutes from Downtown Galveston and the historic The Strand Historic District, where local dining, cafés, shops, and year-round events bring the island’s culture to life. The nearby East End Historic District offers charming streets and a relaxed coastal atmosphere, while the Port of Galveston adds energy and activity as one of Texas’ busiest cruise destinations.

The Landing is a smoke-free community to promote a clean, healthy, and comfortable living environment for all residents. Smoking is not permitted inside apartments or in common indoor areas. Designated outdoor smoking areas are provided and must be used at all times.

Application Process & Leasing

Getting started is easy. Simply click on the “Available Apartments” section and select the apartment you’d like to book. From there, you’ll be guided through our secure, fully online leasing process step by step.

Once your application is submitted, we’ll review it with you and confirm leasing terms and pricing. Credit and background checks are completed online, and if approved, your lease is sent electronically for signature. Payments and required documents are also handled through the same platform—no paperwork and no in-person visits required. In many cases, the entire process—from selecting an apartment to signing the lease and being ready to move in—can be completed in the same day.

The Landing is primarily a community for long-term residents, and our standard lease terms are designed to support stability and a strong residential environment. That said, we understand that individual circumstances can vary. On a case-by-case basis, and depending on seasonality and availability, we may consider shorter-term leases. Lease terms shorter than 12 months may be subject to different rental rates and additional fees. Our team is happy to discuss available options and help determine the best fit based on your specific needs.

Each application is subject to a $25 application fee and a $40 credit and background screening fee, both required for every applicant. These fees are applied consistently and uniformly and are processed securely online as part of the application process.

As part of the application process, each applicant is required to upload a valid government-issued photo ID, two most recent pay stubs, and bank records, as requested during screening. These requirements are applied consistently to all applicants as part of our standard review process. If an applicant does not have the required documentation, alternative options may be considered on a case-by-case basis, subject to verification and approval.

Approval typically takes 24-48 hours after all documents have been submitted.

Roommates are welcome at The Landing. Each adult occupant is required to submit a separate application and complete the same screening process, which is applied consistently to all applicants. Maximum occupancy limits may apply and can vary by apartment type and layout.

Satori Property Management has a well-established working relationship with the local housing authority and accepts housing vouchers at qualifying communities, including The Landing. Voucher applications are reviewed in accordance with program guidelines and our standard screening procedures, which are applied consistently to all applicants. Our management team is familiar with the required documentation and inspection processes and is happy to coordinate directly with Galveston Housing Authority to help ensure a smooth and efficient leasing experience.
