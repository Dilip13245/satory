Welcome Home to The Landing

Welcome to The Landing, a freshly reimagined luxury apartment community in the East End of Galveston—where modern living meets island convenience. Just steps away or a short bike ride from UTMB, downtown Galveston, and local hot spots, The Landing puts everything you need right outside your door.

The Landing offers beautifully designed one-bedroom apartments paired with resort-style community amenities. Enjoy time with friends in the clubhouse, stay active in the fully equipped gym, unwind in the game room, or focus in the study rooms. Outside, you’ll find a refreshing pool, welcoming outdoor spaces, and a dedicated dog park—perfect for both residents and their four-legged companions.

Every apartment at The Landing has been completely renovated in 2026, featuring high-end appliances, modern finishes, and thoughtful upgrades throughout. From stylish interiors to inviting shared spaces, The Landing offers a comfortable, contemporary lifestyle in one of Galveston’s most desirable neighborhoods.

A Trusted Home for Medical Professionals

A preferred medical housing partner.

The Landing is proud to be a preferred housing option for medical staff and healthcare professionals working at UTMB and nearby facilities. With a long history of serving this community, we understand the demands of shift work, long hours, and the importance of coming home to a quiet, comfortable, and reliable place to recharge.

Local, Responsive, and Always Here

Local Management & Maintenance team available 24/7.

The Landing is owned and managed by a local Galveston-based team that lives and works on the island. We understand the unique aspects of the Galveston community—from coastal weather and seasonal conditions to the day-to-day rhythms of island life—and we manage our properties with that local knowledge in mind.

Pet-Friendly Living

We know pets are family.

At The Landing, we know pets are family. We’re proud to offer a pet-friendly community and welcome residents to enjoy their home with their furry companions. With a dedicated dog park and outdoor spaces on site, your pet has room to play while you enjoy island living.

Convenient Parking Options

Hassle-free parking for all residents.

The Landing offers a variety of on-site parking options designed to make daily life easier. Residents can take advantage of covered parking, uncovered parking, and designated visitor spaces, so you—and your guests—always know where to park.

Amenities Designed for Everyday Living

Life at The Landing extends beyond your apartment.

Our community amenities are thoughtfully designed to enhance your daily routine—whether that means starting your morning with a workout, focusing in a quiet study space, or unwinding outdoors after a long day. Residents enjoy access to a fully equipped gym, clubhouse, game room, study rooms, pool, dog park, laundry rooms, and inviting outdoor areas.
