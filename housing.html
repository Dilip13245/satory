Housing Solutions

Customized living options for every need.

1. Corporate Housing

Turnkey solutions for business travelers.

The Landing offers tailored housing solutions for companies and business professionals. Whether you need a fully furnished, turnkey apartment for a short-term assignment or a stable home for a relocating team, our community provides the comfort and connectivity required for professional success.

2. Medical Housing Partnerships

Supporting our healthcare heroes.

As a long-standing housing partner for the Galveston medical community, The Landing is a preferred choice for medical residents, traveling nurses, and UTMB staff. We understand the unique needs of healthcare professionals—from flexible move-in dates to quiet, reliable environments—and we are committed to providing a home that supports their vital work on the island.

3. Experienced Student Housing

Academic focus in a professional setting.

The Landing provides a focused, professional living environment for graduate and medical students. With high-speed internet, dedicated study spaces, and proximity to major academic institutions like UTMB and Galveston College, our community is designed to help students balance rigorous academic schedules with a comfortable island lifestyle.

>

4. Local Partnerships

Connecting community and commerce.

Satory Properties is a local Galveston-based company, and we believe in the power of community. The Landing actively partners with local businesses and organizations to support the island’s economy and provide exceptional housing solutions for employees and guests alike. We are proud to be a dedicated local partner in Galveston’s East End.
